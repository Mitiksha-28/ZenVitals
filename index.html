Our Framework

Energy-Based Wellness

ZenVitals is built on a core belief: your energy is the master currency of wellness. Every dimension of your health — physical stamina, mental clarity, and emotional resilience — is a direct expression of how well you generate, protect, and restore your energy each day.

Physical 💪

Body Energy

Sleep, movement, and nutrition are the three levers of physical energy. When optimised together, they form the foundation all other wellness dimensions rest upon.

Mental 🧠

Cognitive Energy

Focus, clarity, and decision quality are outputs of mental energy. Chronic stress and sleep debt silently deplete this reserve — often before you notice.

Emotional 💙

Emotional Energy

Your mood, motivation, and capacity to connect with others depends on emotional reserves. Low emotional energy manifests as irritability, withdrawal, and reduced resilience.

Recovery 🔄

Recovery Energy

Rest, reflection, and decompression replenish all other energy types. Without intentional recovery, even strong physical and mental inputs yield diminishing returns.

🤖 Artificial Intelligence

Data-driven insight, delivered in real time

ZenVitals' scoring engine applies a weighted input model — energy (×0.3), stress (×0.3), sleep quality (×0.2), and activity (×0.2) — to generate a precise daily wellness score between 0 and 100. Beyond the score, the AI detects emerging patterns across your check-in history to surface adaptive, context-aware recommendations. The more consistently you check in, the sharper the insights become.

🌿 Ayurveda

Personalised through your natural constitution

Ayurveda categorises human physiology into three bioenergetic types — Vata (air & movement), Pitta (fire & transformation), and Kapha (earth & stability). ZenVitals applies these dosha principles as a personalisation layer: your energy patterns, sleep tendencies, and stress responses help identify which constitution influences your current state, allowing recommendations to be meaningfully tailored — not one-size-fits-all.

👆 Acupressure

Targeted techniques for stress and energy

Acupressure is a practitioner-recognised complementary technique based on stimulating specific anatomical pressure points. ZenVitals references clinically studied sites such as LI4 (headache & tension), PC6 (anxiety & nausea), and GV20 (mental clarity & fatigue) to inform targeted intervention suggestions. It is presented as a practical, non-invasive complement to lifestyle change — not a substitute for medical care.
